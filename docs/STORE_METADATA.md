## Launch Full Description

Canopy Trove helps adults discover licensed dispensaries with a calmer, more trusted experience. Every storefront is cross-checked against the New York Office of Cannabis Management's public license registry — so listings that earn the "Verified licensed" badge are matched to a real OCM record, refreshed hourly.

The Verify tab opens a full-screen camera that scans product QR codes and barcodes. Scan a product to see real lab results from NY-licensed testing labs: THC percentage, CBD percentage, terpene profiles, and contaminant pass-fail results (pesticides, heavy metals, microbial, solvents). The tab also supports manual entry: paste any license number, shop name, or street address to confirm on the spot whether a dispensary is licensed in New York. Ideal for travelers, first-time visitors, and anyone who wants to verify a product or shop before stepping inside.

Supported testing labs include Kaycha Labs, NY Green Analytics, ProVerde Laboratories, Keystone State Testing, and ACT Laboratories. Lab information is public data hosted by the labs themselves; Canopy Trove surfaces it for educational purposes and does not certify any test result.

The first Canopy Trove release is built around licensed discovery and product transparency in New York for adults 21+ where lawful. Storefront pages bring together hours, website links, ratings, photos, community reviews, live deals, and the OCM verification signal in one place so the experience feels chosen instead of crowded.

Canopy Trove also includes verified operator tools for licensed storefront teams, with listing management, review follow-up, and visibility controls designed for real businesses. Canopy Trove is not an ordering, checkout, or delivery platform.

Manual entry fallback for license, name, or address lookup.
