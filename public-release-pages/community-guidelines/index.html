Canopy Trove Community Guidelines

Updated: March 29, 2026

Canopy Trove is for lawful storefront discovery and useful storefront feedback. Reviews and reports must stay honest, specific, and safe.

Allowed

  • firsthand storefront experiences
  • factual notes about service, selection, accessibility, pricing, or atmosphere
  • useful corrections about storefront details
  • civil disagreement about a storefront experience

Not Allowed

  • threats, harassment, hate speech, or sexual content
  • instructions for unlawful conduct
  • spam, duplicate reviews, rating flooding, or report flooding
  • impersonation of staff, owners, or other users
  • posting private information, IDs, phone numbers, medical data, or upload documents
  • posting content that is false, defamatory, or clearly manipulative

Moderation Controls

Canopy Trove currently supports:

  • storefront reporting
  • local blocking of review authors
  • manual review workflows for storefront reports
  • manual owner-claim and verification review queues

Enforcement

Canopy Trove may hide, remove, reject, or escalate content that violates these guidelines. Owner verification and storefront reports are intended to move through a manual review path with documented status changes.
