How this works

Canopy Trove storefront links are meant to open directly to the matching storefront record inside the mobile app. This page acts as the hosted fallback when the app is unavailable or direct app-link support has not been completed on the device yet.

Need a correction?

If the storefront opens to the wrong place, does not resolve, or the listing is stale, use the in-app Suggest Edit or Report Closed actions when available, or contact support from the public support page.
