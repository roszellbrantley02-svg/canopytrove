Canopy Trove Terms Of Use

Updated: March 29, 2026

Canopy Trove is a licensed dispensary discovery, storefront-information, and community-feedback product. It is not represented in this build as a direct cannabis sales, ordering, or delivery platform.

Eligibility

Users should only use Canopy Trove where the app and the storefront activity being viewed are lawful in their jurisdiction. Store-review and storefront-discovery features are intended for adults.

User Content

Users are responsible for the reviews, ratings, and reports they submit. Canopy Trove may remove content or suspend access when content violates platform rules, safety standards, or applicable law.

Owner Portal

Owner access is restricted to verified storefront representatives. Claim submissions, identity review, business verification, and owner billing may be limited, paused, or rejected when documentation is incomplete or inaccurate.

No Medical Or Legal Advice

Canopy Trove does not provide medical, legal, or regulatory advice. Storefront information and community content may change and should be independently verified when needed.

Account Controls

Users can manage notifications at the system level and can delete their Canopy Trove account in-app. Subscription and billing management for owners should be handled through the owner billing flow when enabled.

Questions about these terms?

Contact support@canopytrove.com for public release page issues or account-control questions.
