Users should only use Canopy Trove where the app and the storefront activity being viewed are lawful in their jurisdiction. Store-review and storefront-discovery features are intended for adults.

Users are responsible for the reviews, ratings, reports, and any other content they submit. Canopy Trove may remove content or suspend access when content violates platform rules, safety standards, or applicable law.

When photo or media uploads are available, users may only upload content they created or otherwise have the right to share. Users keep ownership of their content, but grant Canopy Trove a limited license to host, display, resize, moderate, and remove that content inside the service. Canopy Trove does not treat member-uploaded photos as owner advertising inventory and will not reuse member photos in Canopy Trove marketing without separate permission.

When photo or media uploads are available, member contributions are treated as user-generated storefront feedback. Users may only upload content they created or otherwise have the right to share, and they are responsible for obtaining consent where a person can be identified.

Canopy Trove may host, display, resize, moderate, remove, or stop displaying submitted media inside the service, and does not guarantee that any uploaded photo will remain live forever. Owner-controlled promotional galleries, storefront hero images, and other operator-managed media are reserved for verified owner workflows and may be subject to additional review, subscription, or placement limits.

Owner Portal

Owner access is restricted to verified storefront representatives. Claim submissions, identity review, business verification, subscription checkout, and billing access may be limited, paused, or rejected when documentation is incomplete or inaccurate.

No Medical Or Legal Advice

Canopy Trove does not provide medical, legal, or regulatory advice. Storefront information and community content may change and should be independently verified when needed.

Account Controls

Users can manage notifications at the system level and can delete their Canopy Trove account in-app. Subscription and billing management for owners is handled through the owner billing flow and billing portal when owner access is approved.
